Who we are?

A Global Cocoa Powder and Coffee Manufacturer based in Indonesia

GreenCocoa Group is a family company and manufacturer, aims to unite the diversity of Indonesian commodities into high value products & brands! In 2000, we started a family-owned cocoa and coffee manufacturer in West Java to spread this blessing to the entire world!

Our Factory

Quality Production Facilities

At our factory, we take pride in turning the best Indonesian cocoa and coffee into top-high quality products. Count on us to meet all your global needs with quality you can trust!

GreenCocoa Factory

Located in West Java, Indonesia, we focus on producing high-quality Cocoa Powder and Coffee products. With production capacity up to 800 metric tons/month, we can supply various products including:
