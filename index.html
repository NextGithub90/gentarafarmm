Who we are?

The Best Agricultural Exporter from Indonesia, Bringing Nature's Finest to the World.

Gentara Farm is the agriculture division of PT Genta Nusantara Group, a forward-thinking company based in Indonesia with a mission to elevate the richness of our local harvests to global markets.

Rooted in the fertile lands of Aceh, we specialize in high-quality coffee, vanilla, and cocoa, grown with integrity and care. Our focus is not only on delivering exceptional agricultural products — but also on building long-term value through sustainability, traceability, and trust.

We work closely with farmers, cooperatives, and local processors to ensure every bean, pod, and crop we export carries the pride of Indonesia and meets international standards. With a vision to become Indonesia's leading agricultural company, Gentara Farm connects local strength with global demand — creating a supply chain that empowers communities and delights the world.

Our Factory

Quality Production Facilities

Rooted in Aceh, Operating Across Key Agricultural Regions in Indonesia

Gentara Farm Factory

Gentara Farm is headquartered in Banda Aceh, Indonesia, serving as the central hub for our strategic and operational activities. As part of our nationwide supply chain, we operate a growing network of sourcing and storage facilities across key agricultural regions of the country.

Currently, our facilities are located in:

  • Aceh Tengah (Central Aceh)
  • Sulawesi
  • West Java
  • Riau
  • Lampung
  • South Sumatra

These locations support the collection, processing, and logistics of our core agricultural commodities — particularly coffee, vanilla, and cocoa — ensuring quality and consistency from origin to export..

This integrated infrastructure reflects our long-term commitment to building a scalable and competitive agricultural export company from Indonesia to the world.

OUR BRANDS

Premium Quality Products

Coffee Produck

Dellaterra Beans is our proudly local coffee brand under the Genta Nusantara Group, created to bring high-quality Indonesian coffee directly to domestic consumers. Every bean we select is a reflection of our deep commitment to flavor, traceability, and sustainability.

With Dellaterra, we aim to raise appreciation for locally produced specialty coffee while maintaining the same level of quality we offer to the international market.

Cocoa Produck
